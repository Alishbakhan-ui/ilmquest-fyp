# IlmQuest 🕌

A gamified Islamic learning application for children aged 5 to 10, built as a Final Year Project (FYP) at Riphah International University, Lahore.

## About

IlmQuest turns Islamic education for children into an interactive adventure. Instead of passive content, children explore worlds, learn through stories, and make moral choices that shape their character, building good *akhlaq* while having fun. Parents stay involved through weekly reports, and all content is scholar-guided rather than user-generated.

## Modules

- **Learning Layer:** structured pre-quest content across five categories (Duas, Kalmas, Surahs, Hadith, and Sunnah), delivered through animated, bilingual (Urdu/English) lessons.
- **Quest Engine:** the gamified core. Two age-targeted, walk-around quests where children move characters by touch, explore scenes, meet NPCs, and make branching moral choices. Junior Quest (ages 5 to 7) uses two-choice moments; Explorer Quest (ages 8 to 10) uses three.
- **Trait & Badge System:** tracks four Islamic virtues (Al-Amin, Sadiq, Adab, Farooq) as percentages, awarding badges as children grow.
- **Parental Dashboard:** auto-generated weekly reports, scholar parenting tips, child profile switching, and a feedback channel.
- **Tutor Directory:** discover admin-approved Islamic tutors with WhatsApp contact and star ratings.
- **Push Notifications:** daily Sunnah reminders, quest completion alerts, and achievement notifications.
- **Admin Dashboard:** platform governance for content, quests, tutor approvals, users, and notifications.

## Tech Stack

- **Flutter:** cross-platform mobile client
- **Bonfire** (built on the **Flame** 2D game engine): quest scenes, character movement, and NPC dialogue
- **Node.js + Express:** REST API backend
- **PostgreSQL (Supabase):** primary database
- **Firebase Auth & Cloud Messaging:** authentication and push notifications
- **Cloudinary:** media storage and delivery

## Access Model

IlmQuest follows a freemium approach. The app is free to install, with free onboarding and the first quest scenarios available to everyone. Premium lessons and later scenarios unlock through an in-app subscription, managed via the Parental Dashboard.
